Economics

PROBLEM: Identify and explain with examples certain areas in your ordinary life and future professional life where knowledge gained from “Economics and Accountancy” will be beneficial. SOLUTION: Why study economics and accountancy? Students who choose to study economics not only gain the skills needed to understand complex markets but come away with strong analytical and problem-solving skills, as well as the business acumen necessary to succeed in the professional world. In fact, economics can be useful for professionals in all industries, not just in business.
